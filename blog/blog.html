Devoloped Daily SMS Schedule

August 2024

In our fast-paced lives, keeping track of daily schedules can be challenging. That's where my Daily SMS Schedule program comes in! This Python-based application automates the process of sending you a text message every morning, detailing your schedule for the day. Let's dive into how this project works and the technologies that power it.

Python Libraries:

  • datetime and calendar: To retrieve current day of the week.
  • smtplib and email.mime.text: To send SMS messages through email gateways

Configuration:

The scheduler pulls essential configurations from a dedicated configuration module. This includes your email credentials and the recipient's number formatted for SMS delivery through various providers.

Weekly Scheduling:

The Schedule module contains a structured dictionary mapping each day of the week to specific tasks. This allows for flexible customization based on your daily needs.

SMS Providers

The Providers module contains information about various SMS gateways, enabling the script to send messages via different service providers.

Future:

Retrieve data from a calendar service to dynamically update your daily tasks.
